To connect to Adobe Analytics data:

1. Select **Adobe Analytics** from the **Power Query - Choose data source** page.

2. Select **Sign in** to sign in to your Adobe Analytics account.

3. In the Adobe Analytics sign-in window that appears, provide your credentials.

4. Enter your password and select **Continue**.

5. Once you successfully sign in, select **Next**.

6. In the **Choose data** page, select the dimensions and measures you want to load, and then select **Transform data** to transform the data in Power Query Editor.
