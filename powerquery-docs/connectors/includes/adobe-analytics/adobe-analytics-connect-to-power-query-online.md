To connect to Adobe Analytics data:

1. Select **Adobe Analytics** from the **Power Query Connect to data source** page.

1. Select **Sign in** to sign in to your Adobe Analytics account.

1. In the Adobe Analytics sign-in window that appears, provide your credentials.

1. Enter your password and select **Continue**.

1. Once you successfully sign in, select **Next**.

1. In the **Choose data** page, select the dimensions and measures you want to load, and then select **Transform data** to transform the data in Power Query Editor.
