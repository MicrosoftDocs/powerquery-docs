To connect to OneStream data:

1. Select **OneStream** from the **Power Query Connect to data source** page.

1. In the **OneStream** dialog, enter the **Environment URL** for your OneStream instance. Include the `https://` prefix.

1. Select **Sign in** to sign in with your organizational account. You're redirected to the OneStream Identity Server to enter your credentials.

1. Once you successfully sign in, select **Next**.

1. In the **Choose data** page, select the functions you want to run (such as Get Cube, Get Custom Adapter, Get Dimension, or Get Member Properties), and then select **Transform data** to transform the data in Power Query Editor.
