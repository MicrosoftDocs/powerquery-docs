To connect to Funnel data:

1. Select **Funnel** from the **Power Query Connect to data source** page.

1. Select **Sign in** to sign in with your Google account or Funnel credentials.

1. Once you successfully sign in, select **Next**.

1. In the **Choose data** page, select the Data Shares you want to load from your Funnel workspace, and then select **Transform data** to transform the data in Power Query Editor.
