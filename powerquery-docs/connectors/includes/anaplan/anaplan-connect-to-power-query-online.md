To connect to Anaplan data:

1. Select **Anaplan** from the **Power Query Connect to data source** page.

1. In the **Anaplan Connector** dialog, enter the **API URL** and **Authentication URL** for your Anaplan instance.

1. Select your authentication kind, and enter your credentials or sign in as needed.

1. Once you successfully authenticate, select **Next**.

1. In the **Choose data** page, select the export actions you want to run, and then select **Transform data** to transform the data in Power Query Editor.
